Physician

Diagnose and treat patients for a variety of medical maladies. Multitudes of specialties,
including cardiology, urology, dermatology, neurology, and gynecology.

Scroll to see your path 

  • Earn your undergraduate degree and pursue a pre-med track in biology, chemistry, or mathematics. Maintain a high GPA.

  • Study for and ace the Medical College Admission Test (MCAT) one year before you plan to begin medical school.

  • Earn your degree from medical school while deciding on a field of study. Great medical schools for primary care include:

    • University of Washington
    • University of North Carolina, Chapel Hill
    • University of California, San Francisco

  • Requirements for internships and residencies will vary based on your field of choice. Most range from three to eight years.

Physicians make great salaries all over, but the highest-paid physicians live in:

  • FLR

    Florence

  • ATL

    Atlanta

  • MED

    Medford

Median salary is

$187,199

per year as of 2014

Opportunities for employment among physicians will increase
9% by 2024, creating 5,100 new jobs.
